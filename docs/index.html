Wynton

UCSF Research Computing at Scale

Wynton is a large, shared storage and computing cluster underlying UCSF’s Research Computing Capability. Funded and administered cooperatively by UCSF campus IT, key research groups and the Bakar Institute, it is available to all UCSF Faculty for their research, and consists of different profiles suited to various biomedical and health science computing needs.

High Performance Computing for All

Wynton is administered using a cooperative model, which provides access to the cluster for all UCSF researchers, regardless of their ability to contribute funds to the system.

Free accounts on the Institutional service tier will receive limited space in Wynton’s storage (BeeGFS distributed parallel filesystem) for their home directory, and access to the large shared working space for job performance. Investigators may contribute additional storage and hardware to the cluster, enabling higher job priorities and more concurrent cores for their tasks. More detail can found here.
