ALL WYNTON UPATES

September 5, 2018

2,000+ CPU Cores and Counting

Wynton today has over 2000 CPU cores available, with several thousand more under test and expected by the end of the year. It is linked to the Pacific Research Platform via a high-speed link. Wynton includes high performance computing and graphical processing profiles, with a next-generation data science profile under test. It is in use by hundreds of researchers from over 40 labs and centers.

August 14, 2017

Wynton is here!

With help from a team of tech savvy individuals coming from a variety of Institutional and Departmental units at UCSF, the Wynton computing infrastructure is now being made available for use in research.
